Can I reschedule an appointment? And how do I go about doing so?

Rescheduling an appointment is really simple. Please give us a call at +65 6123 4567 3 days in advance from the date of your appointment.

Is my payment for treatment with Peach refundable if I want to cancel my appointment?

Peach has an automated procedure with all treatment deposits and payments, which are 100% non-refundable and non-transferable for all bookings.

This is my first time coming to an aesthetic clinic. Is there anything I would need to prepare?

The first time at an aesthetic doctor's can seem daunting. Just come down for a detailed consultation with us! No two people have the same skin, so we will advice you from there.

How many sessions will I need?

The extent and treatment level of your sessions may vary; the number of sessions can range from anywhere between 5 to 8.

How much improvement can I see after a laser treatment?

Expect a general improvement of about 60-90%! Do feel free to speak with us about this during the consultation as this can vary depending on your level of pigmentation.

Can I expect bleeding or downtime for the treatments?

Mild flushing will usually resolve itself after a few hours, as well as small areas of redness for a few days where treatment is most intense.

I am concerned about pain. Are the laser skin procedures painful?

The procedure is actually surprisingly painless. With the numbing cream it feels like a warm and gentle prickling sensation.
